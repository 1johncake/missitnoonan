The basics

Simple rules.
Brutal consequences.

Each animal is assigned to whoever commits the crime. Animals can be passed. Holding an animal at the end costs you some dough.

01
Enter your players
Up to 4 players per game, set them before the game starts.
02
Assign animals as they happen
Three-putt? That's a Snake. Went in the water? Dolphin. Just tap the hole and then tap the animal under the guilty party.
03
Animals move between players
Repeat an offense and the animal transfers to you from whoever held it. Keep it moving.
04
Settle up at 18
Open the summary. Whoever's still holding animals at the end pays out. Hit copy and text the group.
