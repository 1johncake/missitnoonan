Seven Players · Six Rounds · One Champion
I
Race to Seattle
Individual Quota · All Six Rounds

Format: Quota scoring across all six rounds. Each player's quota is 36 minus course handicap. Points earned on gross scores: Eagle = 4pts, Birdie = 3pts, Par = 2pts, Bogey = 1pt, Double bogey or worse = 0pts.

Scoring: Each round, score = total points earned minus quota. Cumulative +/− against quota across all six rounds determines final standings. The player who most exceeds their quota over the trip wins.

Max score per hole: Net double bogey or worse scores zero. No further damage.

Payout:

1st50% of pot
2nd30% of pot
3rd20% of pot
II
Wedghood Champion of the Year
Net Stroke Play · Round 2 · Desert Willow Firecliff

Format: Individual net stroke play, Tuesday morning (Round 2), Desert Willow Firecliff. Gross score minus full course handicap. Lowest net score wins.

Max score per hole: Net triple bogey. Score is capped; no further damage to the card.

Note: The same round also contributes to Race to Seattle quota scoring. One scorecard, two competitions.

Payout:

1st50% of pot
2nd30% of pot
3rd20% of pot
III
The Canyon Cup
Net Match Play Round Robin · All Six Rounds

Format: Net match play, 9-hole matches. Every player faces every other player exactly once across 21 scheduled matches. Groups stay together all 18 holes — matches happen within your group on the Front 9 or Back 9 as drawn. Some nines have no match and are played as skins/stableford.

Match points:

Win2 points
Halve1 point
Loss0 points

Tiebreaker: Head-to-head result, then most wins, then aggregate net score across all matches.

Payout:

1st50% of pot
2nd30% of pot
3rd20% of pot

IV
The Birdie Draw
Raffle · Net Birdies & Eagles · All Six Rounds

Format: A raffle pool funded at the start of the trip. Every player receives one ticket simply for participating. Additional tickets are earned throughout all six rounds based on net scores.

Participation1 ticket (everyone, automatic)
Net Birdie1 ticket each
Net Eagle3 tickets each

Draw: At the closing dinner, tickets are drawn from the pool. Everyone has a chance. The more birdies and eagles you make, the better your odds. One winner takes the pot.

V
Daily Group Winners
High Quota Within Group · Every Round

Format: Each round, the player with the highest quota score within their group wins the daily prize. One winner per group, paid out at the end of each round.

Foursome win8 shells ($24) — 1-in-4 odds
Threesome win6 shells ($18) — 1-in-3 odds

Tiebreaker: Back nine quota score. If still tied, back six, then back three, then 18th hole.

VI
Daily Skins
Net Skins · All Seven Players · Every Round

Format: Net skins across all seven players each round. A skin is won when one player posts the lowest net score on a hole outright. Ties carry to the next hole.

Payout: Fixed pot per round. At the end of each round: pot ÷ total skins won = value per skin.

No skins won: In the unlikely event no skins are claimed, all seven players participate in a 90-foot putting contest on the 18th green. Closest to the hole wins the full pot.
