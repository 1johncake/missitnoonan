Rules

Team Selection

Select 1 player from each Tier to form your team (nine players — one per tier) and submit the winning total score for 72 holes as the tiebreaker. Use the picker app to submit your picks. Text me directly if you have any questions or have trouble: 206-920-1447.

Scoring

The best 4 rounds on your team will be counted each day and this will represent your team score.

Missed Cut / WD / DQ

If a player misses the cut, withdraws, or is DQed, their scores will be equivalent to the cut score (~80).

Overall Prizes

Winner is the team with the lowest total cumulative score at the end of the tournament (before playoffs). Overall second and third place will also be paid out.

Tiebreakers

If there is a tie for first place, there will be a tiebreaker to determine first and second place. First tiebreaker is closest total score to the actual winner before playoffs, high or low. Second tiebreaker is to match the lowest score of the best team player. Third is matching the second-lowest player score on the team, and so on until the tie is broken. If somehow that still does not work, we will split the prize or have a chip off or something.

Daily Prize

There will be smaller payouts for daily low scores. Daily-prize tiebreaker is matching scores for the next (fifth) best player, repeated through all remaining players if needed. If still tied, the prize will be split.

Payout

Payout will vary depending on the number of entries. Daily low score winners plus first, second, and third place will be paid. Check the Message Board for the final payout.

Buy-In

Buy-in is $20 per team. You can have more than one team.

Deadline

Teams and payments are due before 9:00 PM Pacific Time on April 8, 2026.
