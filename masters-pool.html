Rules

Team Selection

Pick 1 player from each of the 9 Tiers. Submit via the picker app on missitnoonan.com — include your 72-hole tiebreaker guess when you submit. Questions? Text 206-920-1447.

Scoring

Your best 4 players each round count toward your team score. Cumulative totals determine overall standings.

Missed Cut · WD · DQ

Player scores +8 per remaining round (approximate cut score).

Prizes

Overall 1st, 2nd, 3rd and 4th place paid out. Daily low team score prize awarded for each round. Payouts scale with number of entries — see Prizes tab for amounts.

Buy-In & Deadline

$20 per team. Multiple entries allowed. Due before 9 pm PT · April 8, 2026.

Tiebreakers

In the event there is a tie for first place, there will be a tiebreaker to determine first and second place. First tiebreaker is closest total score to actual winner before playoffs — can be high or low. Second tiebreaker is to match lowest score of best team player. Third is matching second-lowest score of player on team, and on until the tie is broken. If somehow that doesn’t work, we’ll split the prize or have a chip-off or something. Ties for 2nd, 3rd or 4th are allowed. If more than 4 players are tied, we will use the tiebreaker score to eliminate the number of players necessary to match the number of places; if still tied after the first tiebreaker, we will follow the same process above.

Tiebreaker — Daily

Match the 5th-best player score, then 6th, and so on through remaining players. If still tied, split the daily prize.
