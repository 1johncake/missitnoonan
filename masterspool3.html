Rules

Team Selection

Pick 1 player from each of the 9 Tiers. Submit via the picker app on missitnoonan.com — include your 72-hole tiebreaker guess when you submit. Questions? Text 206-920-1447.

Scoring

Your best 4 players each round count toward your team score. Cumulative totals determine overall standings.

Missed Cut · WD · DQ

Player scores +8 per remaining round (approximate cut score).

Prizes

Overall 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place paid out. Daily low-score prize each round. Payouts scale with number of entries — see Prizes tab for amounts.

Buy-In & Deadline

$20 per team. Multiple entries allowed. Due before 9 pm PT · April 8, 2026.

Tiebreaker — Overall

① Closest predicted 72-hole total to actual winner. ② Match lowest individual player score on team. ③ Match second-lowest, continuing until broken. Last resort: split or chip-off.

Tiebreaker — Daily

Match the 5th-best player score, then 6th, and so on through remaining players. If still tied, split the daily prize.
